About Tech-a-School

At Techalpha Institute, we are committed to bridging the digital divide in education. Our Tech-a-School initiative focuses on providing secondary school students in underserved communities with hands-on training in technology. We aim to inspire and equip them with the tools necessary to succeed in a digital world.

Our Mission

To empower young minds by providing access to high-quality technology education, fostering creativity, and building a foundation for future success in the tech industry.

Program Highlights

  • Comprehensive training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.
  • Workshops on digital literacy and cybersecurity.
  • Mobile app development tutorials and project-based learning.
  • Coding challenges designed to enhance problem-solving skills.
  • Guidance and mentorship from tech professionals.
