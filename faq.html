Frequently Asked Questions

1. What courses does TechAlpha Institute offer?

TechAlpha Institute offers a range of courses, including Front-end Web Development, Back-end Web Development, Web Design with WordPress, Database Management, Mobile App Development, Digital Marketing, Social Media Management, and Content Creation and Management.

2. How do I apply for a course?

You can apply for any course by visiting our admissions page, selecting the course of your choice, and filling out the application form. Once submitted, our admissions team will review your application and get back to you with the next steps.

3. What is the duration of each course?

Course durations vary depending on the subject. Most courses range from 4 to 12 weeks. Detailed information about each course duration is available on the course description page.

4. Are the courses available online or in-person?

TechAlpha Institute offers both online and in-person courses. You can choose the learning mode that best suits your schedule and learning preferences.

5. Do I receive a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, all students who successfully complete their course will receive a certificate of completion from TechAlpha Institute. This certificate can be used to showcase your skills and knowledge to potential employers.

6. What is the cost of each course?

Course costs vary depending on the course you choose. You can find detailed pricing information on our pricing page or by selecting a course on the admissions page.

7. Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in a course?

Some courses may have prerequisites, such as basic knowledge of certain programming languages or tools. These prerequisites are listed in the course description. However, many of our beginner courses do not require any prior knowledge.

8. What support is available to students during the course?

Students at TechAlpha Institute have access to a variety of support resources, including live instructor sessions, discussion forums, and one-on-one mentoring. Our goal is to ensure every student has the support they need to succeed.

9. How do I make payments for the courses?

Payments can be made securely through our website using the Paystack payment gateway. We accept major credit cards and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided during the course enrollment process.

10. Can I get a refund if I need to cancel my enrollment?

Refunds are available under certain conditions. Please refer to our refund policy for detailed information. If you need assistance, you can contact our admissions team for guidance on the process.
